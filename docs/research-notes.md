## Bangalore Rental Market Overview (2026)

### Key trends
- Rental prices increased 18–22% in 2025 across major IT corridors
- Demand continues to outpace supply in prime areas
- 50+ new GCCs (Global Capability Centres) set up in 2025 alone
- Namma Metro Phase 2 now operational; Phase 3 under construction
- More professionals relocating from other cities every year

### Who this is for
- Freshers joining IT companies
- Mid-career professionals relocating
- Employees switching jobs across corridors
- Startup employees in ORR/Koramangala belt
- Anyone planning to move within the city

## IT Corridor Breakdown

### North: Hebbal / Manyata Tech Park
- Companies: Manyata Tech Park, Kirloskar, ABB
- 1BHK: ₹22-28K | 2BHK: ₹38-45K | 3BHK: ₹50-60K
- Pros: Less congested, good road connectivity, cleaner
- Cons: Limited nightlife, far from South/East Bangalore hubs

### East & South (Main IT Belt)

#### Electronic City
- Companies: Dell, HP, Infosys, TCS, Wipro
- 1BHK: ₹18-20K | 2BHK: ₹28-35K | 3BHK: ₹45-55K
- Cheapest major IT hub
- Elevated expressway connectivity
- Cons: Isolated from rest of Bangalore, limited social life

#### Whitefield
- Companies: ITPL, EPIP, GR Tech Park
- 1BHK: ₹25-30K | 2BHK: ₹40-50K | 3BHK: ₹55-70K
- Metro Purple Line improved connectivity
- Strong expat community
- Cons: Feels separate from main city, internal road congestion

#### Marathahalli / Outer Ring Road
- Companies: RMZ Ecospace, ORR tech parks
- 1BHK: ₹20-22K | 2BHK: ₹35-42K | 3BHK: ₹50-60K
- Central location with access to multiple IT hubs
- Cons: One of the most congested areas

#### HSR Layout / Bellandur
- Companies: Embassy Tech Village, ORR south, startups
- 1BHK: ₹22-28K | 2BHK: ₹38-45K | 3BHK: ₹55-65K
- Great food/café culture, well-developed
- Cons: Bellandur flooding in monsoon, rising rents

#### Sarjapur Road
- Companies: Wipro, Infosys
- 1BHK: ₹18-22K | 2BHK: ₹30-38K | 3BHK: ₹45-55K
- Large gated communities, modern amenities
- Cons: Limited entertainment, metro still developing

#### Koramangala
- Companies: Startups, Divyasree, Embassy Golf Links
- 1BHK: ₹30-35K | 2BHK: ₹48-58K | 3BHK: ₹70-85K
- Best lifestyle experience in Bangalore
- Cons: Most expensive, limited new supply

## Rental Laws & Agreements (2026)

### Standard terms
- Agreement duration: 11 months (most common)
- Registration mandatory for 12+ month agreements
- Stamp duty: ~1% of annual rent
- Lock-in period: Usually 3-6 months
- Notice period: 30 days

### Deposit rules (2026 updates)
- New Model Tenancy Code capping deposits being discussed
- Currently still market-determined: 6-10 months standard
- Some co-living/NestAway offering lower deposits (2-3 months)

### Tenant rights
- Landlord cannot cut off essential services (water, electricity)
- Must give 15 days written notice before eviction
- Deposit must be returned within 30 days of vacating
